Modify Web.config
  • Open web.config, below the configuration section: 
        <configSections>
        <section name="ExtAspNet" type="ExtAspNet.ConfigSection, ExtAspNet"/>
    </configSections>

    <!-- other config values: FormMessageTarget="qtip" FormOffsetRight="25" FormLabelWidth="80" -->
    <ExtAspNet Theme="blue" Language="zh_CN" EnableAjax="true"/>
    Parameters in the ExtAspNet section：
    • Theme: Current there are two themes (blue/gray, default:blue).
    • Language: Language (en/zh_CN/zh_TW, default: en).
    • FormMessageTarget: The location where form field error should display (side/qtip, default: side).
    • FormOffsetRight: The offset of form field from the right side (default: 25px).
    • EnableAjax: Whether to enable ExtAspNet Ajax feature(default: true).
    • AjaxTimeout: Ajax timeout(unit:second, default: 30s).


  • Inside the system.web section: 
        <pages>
      <controls>
        <add assembly="ExtAspNet" namespace="ExtAspNet" tagPrefix="ext"/>
      </controls>
    </pages>
    
    <httpModules>
      <add name="ExtAspNetScriptModule" type="ExtAspNet.ScriptModule, ExtAspNet"/>
    </httpModules>
  • done.
